Beef and Barley Stew

Ingredients:

  • Hunters' meat, like venison or boar, obtained with the bravery of a berserker
  • Large onion, chopped into pieces like the debris of a defeated ship
  • Carrots, sliced thick like the timbers of a longship
  • Pearl barley, symbol of tenacity amidst the battle
  • Bone broth, crafted from the remains of your past culinary victories
  • Fresh herbs, like dill and parsley, plucked with the strength of a wild wolf
  • Salt and pepper, akin to the elements of nature in perfect harmony
