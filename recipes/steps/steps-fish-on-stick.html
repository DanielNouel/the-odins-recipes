Fish on Stick

Steps:

  1. In nearby waters, catch a fresh fish that will become your Viking delicacy.
  2. Clean a birch branch to use as your cooking tool. Make sure it's free of impurities and splinters.
  3. With skill, string the fish onto the birch branch. This simple union will be the key to your quick and flavorful meal.
  4. Hold the branch with the fish over the campfire, like a warrior mastering the flames. Cook the fish until it's golden and done.
  5. It's time to savor! Remove the cooked fish from the stick and feel the Viking essence meld with your spirit
