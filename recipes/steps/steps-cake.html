Oat and Dried Fruit Cake

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine oats with dried fruits.
  2. Pour honey over the oat and fruit mixture, uniting all elements with the strength of an unbreakable alliance.
  3. Add fresh milk to the mix, as if nourishing your battle companions before the fight.
  4. Preheat your oven as if stoking the flames of Valhalla. Pour the mixture into a mold.
  5. Place the cake in the hot oven. Let the cake bake at medium heat.
  6. When the cake is golden and firm to touch, remove it from the oven as if receiving a gift from the gods themselves.
