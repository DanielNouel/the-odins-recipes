The Odin's Recipes

Listen well, bold mortals! If you yearn to satisfy your hunger and forge your body like true Viking warriors, you must learn the culinary arts of the gods themselves. Here, I, the mighty Odin, shall guide you through the first of my legendary Viking recipes: The Fish and Roots Soup.

Fish and Roots Soup

Ingredients:

  • Chunks of fresh and brave fish, such as cod or herring
  • Assorted roots, such as carrots, turnips, and parsnips
  • Large onion, chopped into pieces like a defeated foe
  • Cloves of garlic, crushed with the strength of a war hammer
  • Fresh herbs, like thyme and bay leaves, torn with determination
  • Fresh and pure water, drawn from the depths of nature
  • Sea salt, akin to the foam of waves in a storm
